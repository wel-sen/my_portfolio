Empathize

understanding users, business goals, and project constraints through stakeholder interviews, competitive analysis, surveys, and analytics review, resulting in user personas, journey maps, and problem statements

Define

Ideate

multiple solutions are generated through brainstorming, sketching, flow diagrams, and storyboarding, leading to low-fidelity wireframes and concept sketches.

Design

transforms these ideas into interactive models using tools like Figma or Sketch, creating mid- to high-fidelity prototypes and defining UI patterns and component libraries

Test

validate these designs with real users through moderated and unmoderated tests, A/B testing, and qualitative and quantitative analysis, highlighting areas for iteration
